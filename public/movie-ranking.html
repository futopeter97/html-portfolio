My favorite movies of all time

The best movies I've seen as of 2024!

Forrest Gump

The classic story of Forrest Gump, that probably everyone saw once in their life. It's an emotional rollercoaster that never ceases to amaze me, and Tom Hanks was born for this role. If you're curious about his work, and don't know where to start, this is the place.

Interstellar

Nolan's work is known for being abstract, and for a long time Inception was my favorite from his works. However as time went on, and I started to pick apart his movie Interstellar, the closer I got to it. Definitely a movie, that's best on the big screen, but for sci-fi lovers, it's a must watch nonetheless.

Beautiful Boy

I admit, this movie has an important place in my heart, so it's probably not everyone's cup of tea. However I think it depicts it's subject of addiction with great care, through it's heartbreaking story. The cast is also incredible, if you want to see a less comedic side of Steve Carell, you should give this a go.
